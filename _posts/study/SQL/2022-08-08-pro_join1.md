<h2>코드</h2>
```sql
-- 없어진 기록 찾기
SELECT O.ANIMAL_ID, O.NAME
FROM ANIMAL_INS I
RIGHT OUTER JOIN ANIMAL_OUTS O
ON I.ANIMAL_ID=O.ANIMAL_ID
WHERE I.ANIMAL_ID IS NULL
ORDER BY ANIMAL_ID
```
